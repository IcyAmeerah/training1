Replacing Image Holder with Actual Images

Note: When you replace a Smart Object, any scaling, warping, or effects that you applied to the first Smart Object are maintained.

In order to replace images you have to go into the Layerpanel (F7) in Photoshop, find you Layer and right click on it. Select “Edit Contents” (Carefully read note on Photoshop Pop-up window, and click OK).
There You go, you are presented with New document. Insert your picture, edit it as you like and then SAVE IT
